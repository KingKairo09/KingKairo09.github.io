Reflecting on Identity, values, and strengths

Ethical Decision-Making

Two years ago, I received a call from Oranga Tamariki seeking help to house my 16-year-old nephew, a troubled youth with many negative encounters. Living with my dad at the time, I saw it as an opportunity for us to support my nephew together. I accepted the request to re-home him with us. My nephew is dead now, he died earlier this year. I think back to that moment when I got that call and I'm so glad I accepted the request to help my nephew. It was a blessing getting to know him even though his life was short lived he will always be a part of my story.

Cultural Influence on Values and Identity

Raised by a strong mother who valued culture and traditions, I developed a passionate love for my culture, people, and identity. These elements shape my life and influence my actions. I am committed to protecting and honoring them for as long as I live.
