Formulating My Learning Plan

Long-Term Goal or Career Pathway

My long-term goal/career pathway is simply this: I want to invent a website that protects, connects, and saves my people, culture, and traditions.

Strengths and Limitations in Learning

Strengths

A strength of mine that I pride myself on is my ability to teach myself anything! Whether that be a new skill or an unfamiliar technique, I have the ability, patience, and focus required to learn and implement anything I teach myself.

Limitations

A limitation of mine is that I am a slow learner but a thorough one. I often need time to think and understand what I am learning before I can apply it.

Skills to Develop at Dev Academy

I would like to learn how to better manage myself and my time. I often lose interest in what I need to do, which leads me to procrastinate and avoid completing it.

Managing Workload in the Programme

Productive and Safe Collaboration

I am committed to completing this programme because it is vital to my long-term goal. I am committed to coming into campus 3-4 days a week to allow myself to separate my home life and work life so that I can focus and work effectively in a more appropriate environment.

Handling Stress

When things start to build up or get to me, I will "bite the bullet" and work hard. I will take responsibility for my lack of work and strive to work harder. I will get on my own case!

Seeking Help

I will communicate with my facilitators if I need help. I will be open and honest with them and take in any advice they are willing to share with me.

Expectations from the Facilitation Team

I expect them to be open and honest with me!

Scheduling and Commitments

I have commitments to Maurakau (Māori Martial Arts) and Muay Thai (Martial Arts) outside of this course.
