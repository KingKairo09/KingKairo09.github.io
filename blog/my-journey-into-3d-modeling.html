My Journey into 3D Modeling

Kia ora, Fakalofa lahi atu and welcome to my blog! Today, I want to share my journey into the awesome world of 3D modeling.

Introduction

Ko Hikairo tōku ingoa. I've always had a passion for creating hyper-realistic visualizations. Combining my heritage and modern technology has been a driving force in my career.

Discovering 3D Modeling

My journey began a few years ago when I stumbled upon a Donut tutorial about 3D modeling. I was instantly hooked and spent countless hours learning the basics of 3D modeling, texturing, shading, and lighting.

Building My Skills

Over time, I honed my skills and began creating intricate environments and characters. My Cook-Island Māori whakapapa plays a significant role in inspiring my designs, and I strived to bring my cultural elements into my work.

Challenges and Triumphs

The journey wasn't always smooth. I faced numerous challenges, from mastering new software to overcoming creative blocks. However, each obstacle only strengthened my resolve and enhanced my skills.

Looking Ahead

As I continue to grow in this field, I aim to use my skills to contribute to educational initiatives and storytelling projects. I believe that 3D technology has immense potential to engage and inspire audiences.

Conclusion

My journey into 3D modeling has been incredibly rewarding. I'm excited to see where this path leads me and how I can continue to merge my technical skills with my cultural heritage.

Stay tuned for more updates on my projects and insights into the world of 3D modeling. Feel free to leave comments or share your thoughts. Thank you for reading!
